Your vision combines two powerful experiences. Let's break this down to plan our build order.

Two Core Subsystems

1

Landing Page

Brand introduction, value proposition, company showcase. Public-facing marketing presence.

  • Hero section with job highlights
  • About / value proposition
  • Featured companies or categories
  • Call-to-action for job seekers & employers
2

Job Portal

Full-featured job board with authentication, job listings, applications, and dashboards.

  • Job search & filtering
  • Job detail pages
  • User authentication (seekers & employers)
  • Application submission & tracking
  • Employer dashboard

Recommended Build Order

Option A: Landing First

  • Ship something visible quickly
  • Build brand presence early
  • Job portal can follow later
  • Good if you need to attract users NOW

Option B: Portal First

  • Core functionality delivered first
  • Less risk of reworking marketing pages
  • Landing page built around actual features
  • Better if job portal is the main value
